About Me [Google Scholar] [CV] (Updated Jan. 2018)

Zhengming Ding is is now a final-year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Northeastern University, USA, under Professor Yun Raymong Fu. He received the B.Eng. degree in information security and the M.Eng. degree in computer software and theory from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China, in 2010 and 2013, respectively.
