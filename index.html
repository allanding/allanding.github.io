About Me [Google Scholar] [CV] (Updated Jan. 2018)

Zhengming Ding is is now a final-year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Northeastern University, USA, under Professor Yun Raymond Fu. He received the B.Eng. degree in information security and the M.Eng. degree in computer software and theory from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China, in 2010 and 2013, respectively. During my PhD study, I did three summer interns at Microsoft Research (with Yandong Guo and Lei Zhang), Adobe Systems Incorporated (with William Yan), Army Research Lab (with Nasser M. Nasrabadi), in 2017, 2016, 2015, respectively.
